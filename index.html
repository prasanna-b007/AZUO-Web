Our Story

Founded in 2025, AZUO was born from a mission to simplify technology for everyone — from local stores to global enterprises. We believe that powerful software should be accessible to businesses of all sizes.

Today, we're a team of passionate innovators, designers, and engineers working together to create solutions that make a real difference in how businesses operate and grow.
